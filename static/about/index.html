About

I specialize in Virtual Reality, Artificial Intelligence, and trauma-informed research, developing solutions that address real human needs through thoughtful application of emerging technologies and evidence-based social science.

Areas of Focus

Virtual Reality Applications:

  • Therapeutic VR experiences for trauma healing and mental health interventions
  • VR therapy for addiction treatment and anxiety reduction
  • Medical training systems with risk-free simulation environments
  • Elder care solutions addressing social isolation and cognitive health

Artificial Intelligence Development:

  • Self-hosted AI workflow automation with n8n, vLLM, and vector databases
  • Privacy-first local LLM deployment for healthcare and enterprise
  • Autonomous agent-based systems and intelligent automation
  • Research on responsible AI implementation and regulatory frameworks

Research and Social Science:

  • Hypersensitivity as adaptation across marginalized communities
  • Child abuse patterns in religious and high-control groups
  • Trauma-informed technology design and therapeutic applications
  • Intersection of technology, social justice, and healing

Technical Background

  • WebXR development using Three.js, Babylon.js, and A-Frame
  • AI model optimization and production deployment with enterprise-grade inference
  • Research methodology spanning technology, psychology, and social science
  • Evidence-based approach to technology development and therapeutic intervention

I contribute to both technology and social science communities through research publications, open-source development, and advocacy for ethical technology development. My work focuses on ensuring that advanced technologies serve genuine human needs while respecting privacy, autonomy, and dignity-particularly for marginalized and trauma-affected communities.
